Sergi Constance

Sergi Constance, born in 1988 in Valencia, Spain, is fitness model and bodybuilder born in Spain. His passion for powerlifting was ignited during his early years as a student graduating from Sports Science at University of Valencia. The self-made athlete stepped on to the stage for the first time in the Spanish Wabba Cup in 2011, where he scooped the top prize. In 2013 Sergi competed in various shows including Callosa de Segura, the Norway Trophies, and the Bigastro - emerging winner in all three. Apart from his appearances in movies, magazines, and brand ads, Sergi’s millions of followers appreciate his professional ethics, healthy lifestyle, and Be Legend spirit.
